# LILA (ليلى) — Brand Guide

## 1. Winning Product
**Premium Silk Pillowcase — "Mkhadda L'7arer" (مخدة الحرير)**

Why this wins in Morocco:
- **Problem**: Hair breakage, frizz, morning tangles, acne, sleep lines
- **Solution**: 100% Mulberry silk pillowcase — reduces friction, prevents wrinkles, protects hair
- **Price**: Cost $3.50 → Sell 199 MAD ($20)
- **Margin**: ~470%
- **Shipping**: Lightweight (100g), fits any shipping method
- **COD perfect**: Low price point, easy to accept on delivery
- **Viral**: Before/after hair transformations on TikTok Morocco
- **Returns**: Almost zero — non-electronic, universal fit
- **Legal**: Zero trademark issues

---

## 2. Brand Name & Meaning
**LILA (ليلى)**
- Arabic origin meaning "Night" — the time of beauty sleep
- Elegant, feminine, memorable
- Easy to pronounce in Darija, French, Arabic
- Perfect product alignment: pillowcase → sleep → night → LILA

**Tagline Options:**
- "Nharek Katchna B'L'7arer" — Your Morning Shines with Silk
- "Jmalek M'L'Lil" — Your Beauty Comes from the Night
- "Sleep Beautiful" (English for modern feel)

## 3. Logo Concept
```
┌─────────────────────────────┐
│      ✦  L I L A  ✦         │
│    ليلى                     │
│  PREMIUM SILK PILLOWCASE    │
│  مخدة الحرير الفاخرة        │
└─────────────────────────────┘
```

**Logo Variations:**
1. **Primary**: Elegant serif "LILA" with a small crescent moon above the 'I'
2. **Secondary**: Arabic "ليلى" in Thuluth-inspired calligraphy
3. **Icon**: Minimalist silk fold / crescent moon / single star
4. **Monogram**: Interlocked "L" and "A" in gold

**Execution:**
- Hire a Moroccan calligrapher for the Arabic logo
- Use gold foil effect on packaging
- Keep it minimal — Sephora-level elegance

**Usage Rules:**
- Gold for CTAs, icons, dividers, borders
- Cream background for product sections
- Charcoal for headers and footer
- Rose for hearts, feminine accents
- Never use bright/flashy colors — keep it premium

## 5. Typography

### Latin Fonts
| Usage | Font | Weight |
|-------|------|--------|
| Headings | Playfair Display | 400, 600, 700 |
| Subheadings | Cormorant Garamond | 300, 400, 500 |
| Body | Montserrat | 300, 400, 500 |
| Buttons | Montserrat | 600 (uppercase) |

### Arabic Fonts
| Usage | Font | Weight |
|-------|------|--------|
| Arabic Headings | Tajawal | 500, 700 |
| Arabic Body | Tajawal | 400, 500 |
| Darija Copy | Tajawal | 400 |

**Fallback Stack:**
```css
--heading-ar: 'Tajawal', 'Noto Naskh Arabic', 'Traditional Arabic', serif;
--heading-lat: 'Playfair Display', 'Georgia', serif;
--body: 'Montserrat', 'Segoe UI', sans-serif;
```

## 6. Brand Voice & Tone
| Trait | How We Sound |
|-------|-------------|
| Warm | Like a trusted friend giving beauty advice |
| Premium | Never desperate, never "cheap" — we are luxury |
| Moroccan | Darija phrases naturally mixed in |
| Empowering | "You deserve this" not "You need this" |
| Modern | TikTok-native, not traditional |

**Words to Use:**
Jmal (beauty), Nhar (morning), Lil (night), 7arer (silk), Nour (light), Zwin (beautiful), Bnilaa (classy/fancy)

**Words to Avoid:**
Cheap, bargain, discount, budget, save money
