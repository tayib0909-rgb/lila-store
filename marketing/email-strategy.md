## Retargeting Strategy

### Facebook Pixel Events to Track
```
PageView → ViewContent → AddToCart → InitiateCheckout → Purchase
```

### Retargeting Audiences

#### Audience 1: Website Visitors (Last 7 days)
- **Not purchased**
- **Show**: Social proof ad + testimonial
- **Offer**: Free shipping reminder

#### Audience 2: ATC but no Purchase (Last 3 days)
- **Not purchased**
- **Show**: "مخدتك كاتسنا في البانيه" ad
- **Offer**: 10% discount code

#### Audience 3: Purchased Once (Last 30 days)
- **Cross-sell**: "عمري اخترتي هدية لصاحبتك؟"
- **Offer**: Bundle discount (2 pillows = free shipping)

#### Audience 4: Video Viewers (50% of video)
- **Show**: Full video + product link
- **Offer**: Free shipping on first order

#### Audience 5: Instagram Engagers (Last 30 days)
- **Show**: UGC content + customer reviews
- **Offer**: "خصم 10% للفولوورز"

### Lookalike Audiences (after 200 purchases)
- **LAL 1%**: Based on purchasers (best quality)
- **LAL 3%**: Based on ATC (broader)
- **LAL 5%**: Based on video views (reach)

## WhatsApp Marketing Strategy

### Tagging System
| Tag | Trigger | Action |
|-----|---------|--------|
| `new_order` | New order placed | Send order confirmation + tracking |
| `cart_abandon` | 1 hour after ATC | Send WhatsApp reminder |
| `delivered` | Order marked delivered | Check in + ask for review |
| `cod_failed` | COD rejected | Offer alternative (bank transfer) |
| `return` | Customer wants return | Process + ask for feedback |
| `vip` | 3+ purchases | Send special offers |

### WhatsApp Broadcast (Weekly)
- **Friday PM**: أفضل المنتوجات لهاد السيمانا
- **Sunday AM**: نصائح العناية بالبشرة
- **Wednesday**: عروض خاصة للمشتركين
