- **Length**: 30-45 seconds
- **Style**: Authentic Moroccan woman, real house, natural lighting
- **Script**:
  > "I was skeptical too. Every morning — pillow lines, frizzy hair, breakouts. My friend gifted me the LILA silk pillowcase. Now? I wake up with smooth skin, no pillow marks, and my hair actually looks good. It looks bougie but it's actually affordable."

### Ad Format 3: Collection Ad — Bundles
- **Headline**: "اشتري 2 — التوصيل مجانا + هدية"
- **Body**: "مخدة لنومتك ومخدة للغسيل. التبديل كل يوم — والنضارة باينة من أول إستعمال"
- **Creative**: Two LILA pillows in gold packaging, side by side

- **Headline**: "🔥 العرض لنهار فقط: خصم 30% على أول 50 طلبية"

## Campaign Structure (Recommended)

### Phase 1: Testing (Days 1-7)
- **Budget**: 200 MAD/day ($20)
- **Audiences**: 4 interests stacks (test)
- **Creatives**: 3 video variations + 1 carousel
- **Objective**: Conversions (Purchase)
- **Goal**: Find winning ad/audience combo

### Phase 2: Scaling (Days 8-30)
- **Budget**: 500-1000 MAD/day ($50-100)
- **Audiences**: Scale winning interest stack
- **Creatives**: Double down on winning creative
- **Objective**: Conversions
- **LAA**: Create lookalike from first 200 purchases

### Phase 3: Retention (Ongoing)
- **Budget**: 200 MAD/day ($20)
- **Retargeting**: View content (3 days), Add to cart (7 days)
- **Email/SMS**: Flows for abandon cart, post-purchase
